I recommend this any night of the week. It's a really simple recipe too

Credit goes to Rachel Cooper who posted this recipe on the My Recipe Space website. Consider this an abridged version of her recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Herbs - i.e. Rosemary and Thyme
  • Your prefered seasonings - a dash of lemon juice and/or salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Marinade - i.e. mix olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, chopped thyme, etc in a bowl
  2. Marinate Steak - at least 1 hour, if possible
  3. Cook Steak(s) - 5 to 7 minutes each side on a hot grill or pan surface
  4. Let steaks rest

Chop and roast potatoes on the side to really spruce up the dish
