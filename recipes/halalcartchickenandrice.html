This recipe follows her recipe, except for a few ingredient differences

Ingredients

These are her choices. Feel free to use them or to use my simpler recipe and ingredient list below

  • Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • Olive Oil
  • Broth
  • Rice
  • Vinegar
  • Spices (personal preference) - I like salt and pepper

Steps

  1. 1. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  2. 2. Marinate it in a mixture of olive oil, vinegar, and spices. Preferably a few hours. 30 minutes minimum
  3. 3. Heat olive oil in a pan and add the rice
  4. 4. Add chicken broth to the rice and bring to a simmer. Simmer 20 minutes
  5. 5. Cook the chicken in a pan while waitng on the rice
  6. 6. When both dishes are ready, fill your plate and enjoy

you can make your own white sauce and red sauce or use your prefernces

This is a pretty substantial abridged version of Al-Saigh's recipe. She made white sauce from scratch
