Ingredients

  • 1 Ib Fresh Chiles (Jalapeno, Serrano, Habanero, or Ghost, etc.)
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Diced Onion
  • 1 1/2 cups Distiled White Vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Use a food processor or manually puree the chiles, garlic, onions, and salt together into a mixture
  2. Transfer puree to jar and loosely cover at room temp overnight (if you have the time)
  3. Add vinegar, stir, and loosely cover agin (this time 1 to 7 days depending on your preference)
  4. Pour mixture into food processor or blender after you've achieved your desired taste in step 3
  5. Blend until smooth
  6. Store mixture in fridge
